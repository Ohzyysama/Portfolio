**Unique Plane Embedding 唯一平面嵌入**<br>
Every simple 3-connected planar graph has a unique plane embedding.<br>
推论：Every simple 3-connected planar graph has a unique dual graph.<br>
**Colorings of Planar Graphs**<br>
Every loopless planar graph is 4-colorable.
